It is a Google Cardboard game to mesure attention of Children with Autism Spectrum Disorder. Content of game is child travel through railway and see variant objects such as Cars, Cows, Houses that they would be with different combination of colours and animations. In that time the game collects data about what the child interested in. We won two revards in a contest which has over 300 science thesis and we have been many Turkish newspaper with this project.
